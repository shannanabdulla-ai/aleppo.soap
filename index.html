Why Aleppo soap?

Aleppo soap is one of the world’s oldest traditional soaps — centuries of craft using olive oil and laurel bay oil. It's naturally gentle, long-lasting, and biodegradable.

  • Gentle for sensitive skin. Naturally moisturizing from olive oil.
  • Antiseptic & calming. Laurel oil is known to soothe irritation.
  • Eco-friendly. Minimal packaging, natural ingredients, biodegradable.
